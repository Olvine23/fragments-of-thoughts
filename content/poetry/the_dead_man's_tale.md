title: The Dead Man's Tale
date: May 2021
image: /images/dead.jpg
dedication: To the one that couldnt bear the burden of carrying my heart
note: More of times, we tend to give out more than we receive . As much as that is not a bad thing , it gets toxic if effort is not reciprocated 
---

A butterfly flies past me and I wanna fly with it,

But I can't since I am a ghost and we don't do that, 
Besides, even if I could, I won't since I am in pain and regret.
Why? Because I held you in my heart tightly, so badly but you killed me. Or maybe it wasn't your fault, maybe you held my heart too tight, it broke. 
Well either way I'm dead and gone, but my ghost still wanders,
Around and it wonders if, haunting you would make things better.

But again, I can't feel a thing for I lack a heart.
You forcefully snatched it from me but I wonder where you took it. 
Because, Oh My God, you are so heartless and thought less of how I felt.

You never cared but I still trusted you and let you carry my heart.
I fell deeply in love with you not knowing that it was relative to the depth, 
Of my grave which I was never put in for you wanted nothing to do with me, 
Nor the memories we shared so you ordered my cremation, took mine ashes,
Squeezed and crumbled and trashed them in an ocean relatively with how, 
You tore my world apart.

You see, you cause hurt in me, but I loved you, just the way you were. 
I fell for you with no reason, in fact, I fell in love with you the very first day I saw you.
Little did I know that love at first sight follows with a sequence of fights. 
Love is blind, of course it is, I was blinded by your love that I couldn't see what you had, 
On the other side, cruelty.

But I loved you, that's why I saved you as Darling, so that,

So that whenever I heard your ring ring, my heart jumps around.
It jumps thrice causing a thud in its walls awakening the butterflies, 
Which fly around my tummy tickling all of me forcing me to pick up and say, I love you, 
As my first words.

Now I realize that I loved you too much that eventually, you felt nothing.
I loved you, my heart was attracted to yours a lot in that,
Whenever you passed by it bounced hard trying to get out to where you were.
I guess the symphony of yours was pleasing, maybe you got used to mine it irritated you.
And what did you do? Crashed it leaving me, dead and now I'm just but a ghost, 
Wandering in the air to scare you not, that would be childish. 
I wanna haunt you to death so that I can have the last chance with you, because, I am lifeless without you both in death and life.
I promise to be a better man in the afterlife.
