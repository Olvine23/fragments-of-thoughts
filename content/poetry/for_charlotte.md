---
title: A conversation with the stars
date: March 21 2025
image: /images/stars.jpg
dedication: For Charlotte
note: I believe that when a person passes, they just move physically but there is still a connection
--- 

I had a moment with the stars tonight, 
They were alot and bright,
Made me very vibrant and alive
It's been a while since I seen them up 
It was always been cloudy and raining 
Guess the stars were grieving,
Or maybe you were sad for leaving 
And the raindrops were your teardrops 

I felt close to you today , 
I've been taking jumping classes, 
Not for Basketball training but 
Hoping that I can one day jump high enough in the sky and give you a high five,
Trust me, I've literally tried that today. 

Saying I miss you is an understatement, 
It's been hard to eat , sleep since you left 
My routine has shifted from sleeping in my bed to staying out in the dark every night ,
Talking to the stars just so I can get to hear your response . 
Your voice plays on my repeat in my head ,
I crave for your touch that my hand has been numb and pale since you went, 

I hope you are alright though, 
Life's been barely fine,
I still don't have a girlfriend, she'll come with time , 
I envy you for exploring the universe before me ,
Hope you winked at Sirius and said hi to Jupiter as you were passing by 

Hope heaven is warm enough and accomodative,
I hope you never lost your laugh 
As I stay down here I decided that the best I can do is preserve your memory, 
Be the best for you,
And put on smiles on people's faces as you did
